Description

Having close friends is crucial for our well-being. Let's talk about TabLayout that is ViewPager2 close friend.

In a few hours, you have another meeting. You have two things on your mind. One is that you should set up a delivery system with fewer meetings.

The second one is the video about TabLayout that you saw after lunch. It was a continuation of that reference video you used earlier, but you had added it to the long list of unwatched videos.

You know what...tabs are not buttons; they are cooler, and people are more used to them; you can set it to scroll if you want and give custom names for each item. Sure, they are clicky, but they can be swipy.

As you prepare for the upcoming meeting, a realization dawns on you. You have a few hours to revise your presentation and incorporate your new convictions on current ux fashion. You feel confident you can deliver a new talk, but maybe you should rewrite your speech against buttons.

In this stage, you will learn how to work with TabLayout.

Objectives
  1. Place the TabLayout below ViewPager2 with the tab_layout id.
  2. Configure the TabLayout with ViewPager2 via TabLayoutMediator.
  3. Set the resident animal names to TabLayout's tabs.
  4. You can use this documentation link for reference
